BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan)

2013 - Present

Globally Renowned South Korean Boy Band

BTS debuted in June 2013 with the album "2 Cool 4 Skool" and gained international recognition for their distinctive blend of hip-hop, pop, and R&B music genres. They are known for their powerful performances, meaningful lyrics that often touch on social issues, and their strong connection with their fanbase, known as ARMY. Over the years, BTS has achieved unprecedented success, including numerous awards, record-breaking albums, and sold-out world tours. They have also made significant cultural impact, becoming global ambassadors.

Biographies

RM (Kim Namjoon):

  • Role: Leader, main rapper, songwriter.
  • Background: Born on September 12, 1994, in Ilsan, South Korea.
  • Early Life: RM showed an early interest in music and began rapping in middle school. He joined Big Hit Entertainment as a trainee in 2010.
  • Career Highlights: Known for his charismatic rap style and leadership in BTS. RM has contributed significantly to BTS's discography as a lyricist and producer.
  • Trivia: RM is fluent in English and often represents BTS in international interviews and speeches.

Jin (Kim Seokjin):

  • Role: Vocalist, visual.
  • Background: Born on December 4, 1992, in Gwacheon, South Korea.
  • Early Life: Jin studied acting and modeling before joining Big Hit Entertainment in 2011
  • Career Highlights: Jin is known for his powerful vocals and has been praised for his visual appeal. He often participates in writing and composing music for BTS.
  • Trivia: Jin's nickname is "Worldwide Handsome," which reflects his popularity and charming personality.

Suga (Min Yoongi):

  • Role: Lead rapper, songwriter, producer.
  • Background: Born on March 9, 1993, in Daegu, South Korea.
  • Early Life: Suga began his music career as an underground rapper and producer. He joined Big Hit Entertainment in 2010.
  • Career Highlights: Suga is known for his deep and introspective lyrics, as well as his skills in producing music. He has released solo music under the name Agust D.
  • Trivia: Suga's stage name comes from the combination of "shooting guard" (his position in basketball) and the first syllable of "Suga."

J-Hope (Jung Hoseok):

  • Role: Main dancer, rapper, songwriter.
  • Background: Born on February 18, 1994, in Gwangju, South Korea.
  • Early Life: J-Hope was a street dancer and won several dance competitions before joining Big Hit Entertainment in 2010.
  • Career Highlights: J-Hope is known for his energetic dance performances and cheerful personality. He has released solo music and is a key contributor to BTS's choreography.
  • Trivia: J-Hope's stage name reflects his aspiration to bring hope to fans through his music and performances.

Jimin (Park Jimin):

  • Role: Lead vocalist, dancer.
  • Background: Born on October 13, 1995, in Busan, South Korea.
  • Early Life: Jimin studied contemporary dance and joined Big Hit Entertainment in 2011 after being scouted for his dance skills.
  • Career Highlights: Jimin is known for his powerful vocals and captivating stage presence. He is often praised for his dancing abilities and emotional performances.
  • Trivia: Jimin is known for his sweet and caring personality, earning him the nickname "Chim Chim" from fans.

V (Kim Taehyung):

  • Role: Vocalist, songwriter, actor.
  • Background: Born on December 30, 1995, in Daegu, South Korea.
  • Early Life: V showed an interest in music and acting from a young age. He joined Big Hit Entertainment in 2011.
  • Career Highlights: V is known for his deep, soulful voice and has acted in various dramas and films. He has also released solo music.
  • Trivia: V is known for his unique fashion sense and is often described as mysterious and enigmatic by fans.

Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook):

  • Role: Main vocalist, rapper, dancer, maknae (youngest member).
  • Background: Born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea.
  • Early Life: Jungkook excelled in sports and music during his school years. He joined Big Hit Entertainment after being scouted for his singing talent in 2011.
  • Career Highlights: Jungkook is known for his powerful vocals, impressive dance skills, and versatility. He has released solo music and is highly regarded for his stage presence.
  • Trivia: Jungkook is known for his competitive spirit and passion for learning new skills, earning him the nickname "Golden Maknae."
